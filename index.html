About Myself

Hello, my name is Fan Li (李凡), a master student at Tongji University, welcome to my website! Right now I major in Mechatronic Engineering. My current research interests mainly focus on the perception and motion planning for excavator robots supervised by Dr. Xiaotian Li and Prof. Anlin Wang.

Prior to this, I graduated from Tongji University in Shanghai, China in 2021 with a bachelor’s degree in mechanical engineering (Mechatronics), and seized the award of Excellent Graduates in Shanghai. During my undergraduate studies, I mainly focused on drone visual tracking under the supervision of Prof. Changhong Fu in Vision4Robotics Group. My bachelor’s thesis is about motion planning for excavator robots supervised by Dr. Xiaotian Li. Besides, I am closely working with Prof. Peng Lu from ArcLab @ HKU.
